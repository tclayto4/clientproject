About Calculated Car Audio Creations

Calculated Car Audio Creations started as a web development project with the simple goal of easing the process of installing car audio systems.

Our Goal

Our goal of this website is to provide the necessary tools and reasources for car audio installations with the purpose of making it soemthing that anyone is able acomplish.

About the Designer

This website is designed and developed by Thomas Clayton, a twenty year old computer science student with a pasison for bass and anything DIY.

What's Next?

Planned future features include interactive box design calculators, wiring diagrams, and a gallery of real-world projects to inspire ideas for new builds.
